Welcome to Montana!

"Yellowstone" follows the powerful Dutton family, owners of the largest contiguous ranch in the United States. Led by patriarch John Dutton, they navigate the challenges of preserving their land, wealth, and legacy in the face of encroaching developers, politicians, and Native American tribes. The show delves into the complex dynamics of family loyalty, betrayal, and the rugged landscape of Montana's Yellowstone Valley. As tensions escalate and conflicts arise, the Duttons must confront their adversaries while protecting what is theirs, amidst a backdrop of stunning vistas and intense drama.

Fun Fact!

Yellowstone is filmed on location in Darby, Montana at the Chief Joseph Ranch. You can book a stay at the ranch in one of the cabins which are also featured on the show as residences of the Duttons.

In the News

The Final Season is Near

The foremost drama on television mirrors the tumultuous dynamics within the television industry itself. Despite Paramount Network's acclaimed Western series, Yellowstone, poised for a smooth ascent to record-breaking viewership, its journey resembles that of an untamed bronco, fiercely resisting control.

Another Spinoff Coming?

Hold your horses, Yellowstone enthusiasts. It appears the Matthew McConaughey-led spinoff might not have left the stable just yet. While initial reports indicated a sequel series would promptly follow Yellowstone's series finale in November 2024, a recent update suggests McConaughey's participation isn't a guaranteed certainty after all.

Kevin Costner Wants Back In!

Throughout much of 2023, Kevin Costner dominated headlines due to his dispute with the producers of Yellowstone, ultimately leading to his exit from the immensely popular series before its conclusion. A recent report suggests Costner is interested in returning for the show's upcoming final episodes — but whether the show shares this sentiment remains uncertain.

The REAL Cowboys of Yellowstone

Taylor Sheridan

Creator, Character - Travis Wheatley

Yellowstone creator Taylor Sheridan is also an actual cowboy in real life. In fact, the creation of Yellowstone is based on Sheridan’s own childhood in Texas, adding some authenticity to the show’s plot.

Forrie J. Smith

Character - Lloyd

Smith has had years of cowboy, horse riding, and rodeo experience before his recurring role as Lloyd in Yellowstone. Growing up riding horses at his grandparents’ ranch in Yellowstone’s setting of Montana, Smith is more than qualified as a cowboy.
